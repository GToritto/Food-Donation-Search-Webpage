About Golden Plate

Learn more about Golden Plate and our mission to connect food banks, shelters, and restaurants with donors like you. Golden Plate aims to connect local food banks, shelters, and restaurants with individuals or businesses looking to donate food. Schedule a pick-up or track your donations effortlessly. Let's work together to address food insecurity in our community.
